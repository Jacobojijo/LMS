2.3.2 Animals

In Ekegusii, animals are classified based on their species. Below is a detailed list of animals categorized by their types:

Wild Animals:

  • Endo– Lion
  • Engubo– Hippopotamus
  • Enchogu – Elephant
  • Engo – Leopard
  • Rigwari – Zebra
  • Esirori– Giraffe
  • Eng’iti– Snake
  • Embeche– Warthog
  • Egekondo– Monkey
  • Embuni – Ostrich
  • Ekeobiri – Rhinoceros
  • Eng’era – Buffalo
  • Egechure – Gazelles
  • Egesongo– Antelope/Impala
  • Enkuru– Tortoise
  • Emamba– Crocodile
  • Edubu– Ant-bear
  • Egesusu – Rabbit/Hare
  • Embeba– Rat
  • Egesambeba– Squirrel
  • Eng’uko – Mole
  • Enyang’au– Hyena
  • Obotigori– Fox
  • Egesimba – Wild Cat

4. Birds (Enyoni)

Birds in Ekegusii are also categorized based on their species. Below is a list of various bird species:

  • Ekebaki – Hawk
  • Omonyaroro– Swallow
  • Ekong’a– Crane
  • Richore – Weaver bird
  • Enchiro – Quail
  • Egesibi – Sunbird
  • Riruma– Dove
  • Enyakweumi– Owl

5. Fish (Enswe)

Fish in Ekegusii are categorized by species, many of which are commonly found in the region's lakes and rivers.

  • Eng’ege– Tilapia
  • Engonye – Mudfish
  • Kamongo– Cut fish
  • Omena – Elastis
  • Enyangi – Nile perch
  • Okoko – A kind of fish with spines on the sides

6. Domestic Animals (Etugo)

Domestic animals are essential in Ekegusii culture, serving various roles, such as providing milk, meat, and companionship.

  • Eng’ombe – Cow
  • Eeri– Bull
  • Embori– Goat
  • Eng’ondi– Sheep
  • Emanu – Ram
  • Ritororo– Heifer

7. Poultry

Poultry includes domesticated birds kept for food, eggs, or feathers.

  • Engoko – Hen
  • Etuoni– Cock
  • Egechuchu– Poullette (Young hen)
  • Embata– Duck
  • Riruma – Dove

8. Plurals of the Nouns

In Ekegusii, the plural forms of animals and birds often follow specific patterns. Here are examples of singular and plural forms:

Domestic Animals (Etugo):

  • Esese – Dog → Chisese– Dogs
  • Ekebusi – Cat → Ebibusi– Cats
  • Eng’ombe– Cow → Chiombe– Cows
  • Emanu– Puppy → Chimanu – Puppies

Summary:

Animal Categories:

  • Wild Animals: Include lions, elephants, giraffes, leopards, snakes, and more.
  • Birds: Hawks, doves, owls, cranes, and quails.
  • Fish: Tilapia, trout, and Nile perch are some examples.
  • Domestic Animals: Include cows, goats, sheep, and poultry (chickens, ducks)

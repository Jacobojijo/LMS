Advanced Dholuo Language Course

Course Overview

The Advanced Dholuo Language Course is designed to deepen the linguistic skills and cultural understanding of students who have completed the beginner level. This course focuses on advanced grammar, complex sentence structures, cultural nuances, and fluency in both written and spoken Dholuo.

Duration

2 Months (8 Weeks)

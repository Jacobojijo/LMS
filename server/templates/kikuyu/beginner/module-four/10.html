4.5.3 The Compliment of Aim, Cause & Locality

A. The Complement of Aim

1. Definition and Communicative Function

Shows the intention or purpose of an action.

➡️ It answers: "For what purpose?" / "Why are we doing this?"

2. Structural Formula (Kikuyu)

Subject + Verb + kĩa/cĩa + Aim

  • The marker kĩa / cĩa means: for (purpose).

3. Semantic Role

Links an action to its goal / aim.

4. Examples (Kikuyu)

Kikuyu Sentence Meaning Notes
Tũratũma mbeca cĩa gũcookia ciana cukuru. We are sending money for taking the children to school. cĩa = for (purpose)
Ndĩ kũhũũra nguo cĩa rũgendo. I am washing clothes for a trip. Shows intention
Nĩathondekete gũciaro kĩhũa kĩa kũthinga mũgũnda. He built a fence to protect the farm. Action → goal

💡 kĩa / cĩa expresses purpose (NOT possession).

B. The Complement of Cause — (Gũcaria Kĩhĩĩo / Reason Complement)

1. Definition

Shows the reason or cause of an action.

➡️ Answers: "Why did it happen?"

2. Structural Formula (Kikuyu)

Main Clause + nĩ ũndũ wa + Cause

  • The phrase nĩ ũndũ wa = because of / due to.

3. Examples

Kikuyu Sentence Meaning Notes
Ndikũthiĩ gũtũũra nĩ ũndũ wa mbura. I will not go settle because of rain. Shows cause
Mũrutani ũcio nĩ mũrakaru nĩ ũndũ wa mĩceno. That teacher is angry because of noise. Cause identified
Gĩkundi kĩu nĩkĩgathũkire nĩ ũndũ gũtirĩ mũgũnda. The group dissolved because there is no money. Logical causal link

💡 nĩ ũndũ = because of (reason)

C. The Complement of Locality — (Gũcaria ũhoro wa Kũhingũra / Place Complement)

1. Definition

Shows where someone or something is located.

➡️ Answers: "Where?"

2. Structural Insight

The location comes directly after the verb, without adding "in/at".

Subject + Verb + Location

3. Examples (Kikuyu)

Kikuyu Sentence Meaning Notes
Ndĩ Nairóbi. I am in Nairobi. No preposition used.
Tũciarĩa Nyeri. We live in Nyeri. Direct locality.
Mũgunda wĩ Gachatha. The farm is in Gachatha. Location as complement.

4. Interrogatives

Kikuyu Question Meaning
Wĩ kũu? Where are you?
Mũciarĩa kũrĩ? Where do you all live?
Ndĩ haha. I am here.

💡 No "in/at/on" is inserted — Kikuyu uses bare location.

D. Comparative Overview: Aim vs Cause vs Locality

Complement Type Marker Guiding Question Function Example (Kikuyu)
Aim (Purpose) kĩa / cĩa For what? Shows intention Ndĩ kũhũũra nguo cĩa rũgendo.
Cause (Reason) nĩ ũndũ wa Why? Gives reason Tũrenda kwarora nyũmba nĩ ũndũ wa rũgendo.
Locality (Place) (no preposition) Where? Indicates location Ndĩ Nairóbi

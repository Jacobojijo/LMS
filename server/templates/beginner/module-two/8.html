2.3.2 Animals

In Dholuo, animals are classified based on their species. Below is a detailed list of animals categorized by their types:

Wild Animals (Sibuor, Rawo, etc.):

  • Sibuor – Lion
  • Rawo – Hippopotamus
  • Liech – Elephant
  • Kwach – Leopard
  • Magwar Okanga – Zebra
  • Tiga Ng'ute bor – Giraffe
  • Thuol – Snake
  • Mbidhi – Warthog
  • Ong'er – Monkey
  • Udo – Ostrich
  • Omuga – Rhinoceros
  • Jowi – Buffalo
  • Nyakech – Gazelles
  • Muanda – Antelope/Impala
  • Opuk – Tortoise
  • Nyang' – Crocodile
  • Liwinji – Tiger
  • Muok – Ant-bear
  • Apuoyo – Rabbit/Hare
  • Oyieyo – Rat
  • Ayidha – Squirrel
  • Fuko – Mole
  • Otoyo – Hyena
  • Kibwe – Fox
  • Ogwang' – Wild Cat

4. Birds (Winy)

Birds in Dholuo are also categorized based on their species. Below is a list of various bird species:

  • Otenga – Hawk
  • Opija – Swallow
  • Owang' – Crane
  • Osogo – Weaver bird
  • Aluru – Quail
  • Onyodhi – Sunbird
  • Akuru – Dove
  • Tula – Owl

5. Fish (Rech)

Fish in Dholuo are categorized by species, many of which are commonly found in the region's lakes and rivers.

  • Ngege – Tilapia
  • Ningu – Trout
  • Kamongo – Mudfish
  • Mumi – Cut fish
  • Omena – Elastis
  • Mbuta – Nile perch
  • Okoko – A kind of fish with spines on the sides

6. Domestic Animals (Chiaye)

Domestic animals are essential in Dholuo culture, serving various roles, such as providing milk, meat, and companionship.

  • Dhiang' – Cow
  • Ruath – Bull
  • Diel – Goat
  • Rombo – Sheep
  • Yim – Ram
  • Roya Buong' – Heifer

7. Poultry

Poultry includes domesticated birds kept for food, eggs, or feathers.

  • Gueno – Hen
  • Thuon – Cock
  • Nyaburi – Hen
  • Swi – Poullette (Young hen)
  • Atudo/Oyoyo – Duck
  • Akuru – Dove

8. Plurals of the Nouns

In Dholuo, the plural forms of animals and birds often follow specific patterns. Here are examples of singular and plural forms:

Domestic Animals (Chiaye):

  • Guok – Dog → Guogi – Dogs
  • Paka – Cat → Pekni – Cats
  • Dhiang' – Cow → Dhok – Cows
  • Nyaguok – Puppy → Nyiguogi – Puppies

Summary:

Animal Categories:

  • Wild Animals: Include lions, elephants, giraffes, leopards, snakes, and more.
  • Birds: Hawks, doves, owls, cranes, and quails.
  • Fish: Tilapia, trout, and Nile perch are some examples.
  • Domestic Animals: Include cows, goats, sheep, and poultry (chickens, ducks)

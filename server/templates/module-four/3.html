4.1.4 Words "Donge", "Bende" and "Nang'o"

Each of the Q-words [donge] 'Isn't it the case that...?' [bende] 'Is it the case that...?', and [nang'o] 'Why?' iss a single-word constituent with its own semantic role as a Q-element. In terms of constituent order, they are all free constituents, not capable of any structural commitment. Moreover, like [ok], they usually have the whole predication their scope. Howeve8r, like all other free constituents so far discussed, they have certain pattern restrictions which the expression rules handling their surface positioning must respect. Let us look at each of them in turn.

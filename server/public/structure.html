Advanced Dholuo Language Course

Course Overview

The Advanced Dholuo Language Course is designed to deepen the linguistic skills and cultural understanding of students who have completed the beginner level. This course focuses on advanced grammar, complex sentence structures, cultural nuances, and fluency in both written and spoken Dholuo. Over two months (8 weeks), students will refine their language abilities, preparing them to use Dholuo confidently in various professional, academic, and cultural settings.

Duration: 2 Months (8 Weeks)

Weekly Lessons: 5 lessons per week (Monday-Thursday: 5:30 AM - 6:30 AM; Sunday: 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM)

Course Structure

1. Module 01

Week 1-2: Advanced Grammatical Categories and Structures

2. Module 02

Week 3-4: Advanced Grammatical Categories and Complex Sentence Types

3. Module 03

Week 5-6: Predicate Frames, Expression Rules and Complex Sentences

4. Module 04

Week 7-8: Advanced Communication Skills, Cultural Proficiency and Mastery

Continuous Assessment Tests (CATs)

  1. CAT 1 (End of Week 2): Advanced Grammatical Categories and Structures. (20%)
  2. CAT 2 (End of Week 4): Advanced Grammatical Categories and Complex Sentence Types (20%)
  3. CAT 3 (End of Week 6): Predicate Frames, Expression Rules and Complex Sentences (20%)
  4. Final Exam (End of Week 8): A comprehensive test covering all advanced topics, including writing, speaking and interpretation. (40%)
